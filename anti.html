ANTI-DICTIONARY


This project was to explore the conceptual matrix surrounding Georges Bataille's notion of formlessness. How to create a system that is not based on logical patterns? How can graphic strategies and outcomes that is not complete, not sequential, not organized, and not rational? Entries from the critical dictionary, Encyclopaedia Acephalica, were combined with self-generated texts, graphics, and typography in reflection of the each entry to produce a document; an anti-dictionary.



PROCESS:

Purpose - Critique, Dialogue
What is it - Critical Dictionary, Typography, Imagery
Instructor - Geoff Kaplan and Stuart Kendall
