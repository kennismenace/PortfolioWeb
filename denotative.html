DENOTATIVE


This project was to design a logical, efficient articulation of an instructional manual. Designing in a high modern style and focusing on clarity of hierarchy between typographic levels.

Denotative definition: Specific, direct, in accordance with fact or primary meaning, non-interpretive. In other words, present the information in an objective manner.



PROCESS:

1. Research reference materials on modernist typography.
2. Analyse the given texts and images.
3. Sketches of rough variations of possible layout solutions to the content.
4. Typography decisions - typeface, size and grid structure based on the content, and how things necessitate being grouped.

Purpose - Denotation, Systematic
What is it - Typography, Illustration, Instructional Manual
