Hello, I'm Kennis Chow! I'm a graphic designer currently pursuing my Master of Fine Arts in Design at California College of the Arts. After receiving my BFA from Emily Carr University of Art + Design, I worked as an illustrator and participated in group exhibits in Canada and the United States. In my studies, I explore storytelling through experimentation and research. I challenge myself to produce across various media and develop a detailed process that creates both informative and inspiring visual narratives.


I'm always looking for opportunities to expand my knowledge as a human being and as a designer! :)
