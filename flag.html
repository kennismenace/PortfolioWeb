ANATOMY OF A FLAG


This book is restructured, redesigned, and reinterpreted from a booklet called “Good Flag, Bad Flag: How to Design a Great Flag” by Ted Kaye from the North American Vexillological Association. Using the book as the source content to restructure, redesign, and reinterpret using conditions and constraints that I author to create a sympathetic, conceptual structure.

Sympathetically definition: Of, expressing, feeling, agreeably suited to, in concord with meaning.



PROCESS:

1. Read booklet.
2. Analyse source material, content, and visuals.
3. Develop visual directions.
4. Sketches of rough variations of possible layout, structure and binding solutions to the content.
5. Develop a palette of potential typefaces.
6. Typography Decisions - Typeface, size and grid structure based on the content, and how things necessitate being grouped.

Purpose - Restructure, Redesign, Reinterpret
What is it - Typography, Book Design
