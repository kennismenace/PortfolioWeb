CONNOTATIVE STEAM IRON


Using the content from the denotative steam iron's instructional manual project to create a connotative steam iron poster. This poster is expressive, painterly, emotive, illustrative, and as well as both typographical and experimental. Does it spin, emit sound, bend or remain still? Is it large or small? Quiet or loud? Is it associated with an action or emotion? The answers to these questions helps to communicate through typography and determine the characteristics that guide the design decisions.

Connotative definition: Suggested meaning that needs to be interpreted. Something suggested by a word or thing, implied meaning. The set of associations implied by a word in addition to its literal meaning, or having the power of implying or suggesting something in addition to what is explicit.
